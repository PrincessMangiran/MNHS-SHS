ABOUT US

At Masville National High School-Senior High School, the school is good, and the teachers are good at teaching, and the students are very responsible for their academic performance. Has have a Strands HUMSS, TVL and ABM Strands to a Grade 11 & 12 students. Grade 11 has have a six section. In Grade 12, students also have five sections. 

Strand Track

MNHS-SHS have a two track Accademic track & Technical Vocational Livelyhood that has four strand HUMMS, ICT, ABM, TVL.

ACADEMIC TRACK

1.) Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) - is a mean of analyzing a company's profitability by looking at each aspect of it's business to determine strenghts and weaknesses.

2.) Humanities and Social Sciences(HUMMS) - It is a program or course of study that focuses on subjects such as literature, history, philosophy, sociology, and other social sciences.

TECHNICAL VOCATIONAL LIVELYHOOD

1.)Information and Communication Technology(ICT) - covers all technical means used to handle information and aid communication. This includes both computer and network hardware, as well as their software.

2.)Technical Vocational Livelyhood(TVL) - is designed to develop students' skills that is useful for livelihood and technical projects. It provides a curriculum that is a combination of Core Courses and specialized hands-on courses that meets the competency-based assessment of TESDA.
